Doposcuola 2025

A journey of inclusion, care, and growth for the little ones

Introduction – Where Children Smile Again

Every afternoon at 3:30 PM sharp, a door opens in the heart of Rutigliano. It is not just the entrance to a classroom or building: it is an entry to a space where dreams are nurtured, bonds are formed, and trust is ignited...

Who We Are

The DOPOSCUOLA 2025 project was initiated by the Parish of San Domenico di Guzmán in Rutigliano (BA)...

Context

In the area of Rutigliano, more and more primary school pupils, especially of foreign origin...

Who We Help

Currently, the project serves 17 primary school children, divided as follows:

  • 10 girls and 7 boys
  • 7 in the 2nd grade, 8 in 3rd grade, 1 in 4th grade, and 1 in 5th grade
  • 13 of Albanian origin and 4 Italian

Many of them experience linguistic and cultural disadvantages...

The Heart of the Project

Doposcuola 2025 is much more than just homework help: it is an educational, relational, and community space...

Activities and Methodology

  • Homework help in small groups with personalized educational tutoring
  • Linguistic and cognitive workshops
  • Creative and manual activities such as clay modeling
  • Expressive workshops: theater, mime, dance
  • Intercultural paths to value differences
  • Active involvement of families

The Strength of Volunteering

  • 5 adults from MASCI
  • 2 early childhood education students
  • 1 IT specialist
  • 13 teachers (1 active, 12 retired)

Organization and Timing

  • 5 days a week
  • Hours: 3:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Expected Impact

  • Provide effective school support
  • Prevent early school dropout
  • Strengthen the school-family relationship
  • Promote cultural and social integration
  • Enhance local traditions

Sustainability and Future Vision

The project aims to become a stable and replicable educational model...
